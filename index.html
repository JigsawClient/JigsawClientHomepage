What is Jigsaw?

Jigsaw is a Minecraft hacked client that can switch between being a ghost client and a normal client. It has the most stable infinite reach of all. Currently, Jigsaw is under development but you can still download it. Jigsaw is being developed by me, a 15 year old boy from Sweden. I love coding and this is my biggest project to date. The client is a bit messy and complex at the moment, so it may be hard to start using it. I am working on making it more user-friendly by adding more commands and better tooltips. I may also do tutorials.
